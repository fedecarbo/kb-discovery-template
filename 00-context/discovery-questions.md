## How to use this file

- Every interview guide (`guide.md` in a session folder) should target one or more of these questions explicitly.
- The session `interview.md` header should list the Q-IDs it targets.
- When a question moves to high confidence (multiple independent sessions converging), move it to "Answered or retired" with a one-line summary.
- When a question stops being useful (replaced by a sharper one), retire it rather than keeping it for completeness.
- The **Coach role** (`.claude/skills/coach/`) proposes deltas to these questions based on past interviews; proposals land in `00-context/method-notes.md`. Apply the ones you agree with by direct edit.
