## How to use this file

- Every interview guide (`guide.md` in a session folder) targets one or more of these questions explicitly.
- The session `interview.md` header lists the Q-IDs it targets.
- When a question reaches high confidence (multiple independent sessions converging), move it to **Answered or retired** with a one-line summary.
- When a question stops being useful (replaced by a sharper one), retire it rather than keeping it for completeness.
- The **Coach** role (`.claude/skills/coach/`) proposes deltas to these questions based on past interviews. Proposals land in `01-research/method/coaching-log.md`. Apply the ones you agree with by direct edit.
