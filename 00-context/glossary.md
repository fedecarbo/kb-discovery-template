# Glossary

> *What this file is: a reference encyclopedia for this project. Each entry
> describes what an entity *is* — its role in the ecosystem, why it matters
> to the discovery work, and any context that helps interpret what
> participants say about it. Consult this before reading transcripts and
> synthesis; if a name is unfamiliar, look here first.*
>
> ASR (auto-transcript) correction notes are included where relevant but are
> secondary — the primary purpose is *understanding*, not transcript-fixing.
>
> A separate **§ Your review queue** at the bottom holds items flagged during
> transcription or synthesis where the term couldn't be confirmed from the
> transcript alone. Normal resolution: check the recording (if available),
> graduate the item into a full entry above, or ask the participant or a
> domain expert at next contact.
