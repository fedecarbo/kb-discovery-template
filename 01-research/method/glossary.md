# Glossary

> [!NOTE]
> **What this file is.** A reference encyclopedia for this project. Each entry describes what an entity *is* — its role in the ecosystem, why it matters to the discovery, and any context that helps interpret what participants say about it. Consult before reading transcripts; if a name is unfamiliar, look here first.

> [!TIP]
> ASR (auto-transcript) corrections appear where relevant, but they're secondary. The primary purpose is **understanding**, not transcript-fixing.

The **Review queue** at the bottom holds items flagged during transcription or synthesis where the term couldn't be confirmed. Resolve by checking the recording, graduating the item into a full entry above, or asking the participant or a domain expert at next contact.
