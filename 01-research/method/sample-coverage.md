## Why this matters

A claim like *"X unlocks Y"* might be universal. Or it might only be true for users with property Z. You can't tell which until you know *who said it*.

This file makes the shape of the sample visible, so you can tell the difference between a strong insight and a strong insight *in a thin slice*.
