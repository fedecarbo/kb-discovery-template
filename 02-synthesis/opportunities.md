## Chain-of-evidence rule

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Every opportunity **must cite ≥1 insight ID**. Every prototype or test must cite ≥1 opportunity. Every decision must cite ≥1 piece of evidence (insight, opportunity, or test).

This is what stops the project quietly drifting away from what participants actually told us.

If an opportunity can't be traced back to a real insight, either it isn't ready or there's a missing insight that should be written first.
