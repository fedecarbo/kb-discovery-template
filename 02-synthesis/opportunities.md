## Chain-of-evidence rule

**Every opportunity must cite ≥1 insight ID** it's rooted in. Every prototype or test must cite ≥1 opportunity. Every decision must cite ≥1 piece of evidence (insight, opportunity, or test). This is what stops the project quietly drifting away from what the founders actually told us.

If an opportunity can't be traced back to a real insight, either it isn't ready, or there's a missing insight that should be written first.
